Our CEO

My name is Davi Freitas. I’m from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and in 2025 I moved to Rexburg, Idaho, to study at BYU-Idaho. Back in Brazil, I was blessed to share my life with several dogs who filled my days with love and companionship. One of my greatest companions was Kinder — she was full of energy, very playful, and loved playing tag.

When I moved to the United States, my busy life didn’t allow me to care for a pet, and I deeply missed Kinder and my other best friend, Mile. This longing inspired me to create this site — a place where people who love animals can connect, share moments of joy and affection, and experience the special friendship between humans and their four-legged friends. Because no matter how busy life gets, the love for pets should never fade.
